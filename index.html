Our Story

89 years of powering the Shore

Good Friend Electric was founded in 1937 by Manny and Lillian Goodfriend, on Main Street in downtown Toms River. In 1949 Irving Rosen purchased the business, and by 1957 the company was incorporated as Good Friend Electric — the beginning of four generations of family ownership.

Today the company is led by President David Rosen and his son Ethan Rosen, Sales & Operations Manager. Our associate team collectively brings more than 350 years of industry experience to every counter, every order, and every job site.

From a single downtown storefront to six full-service branches across Ocean & Monmouth County, one thing hasn't changed: when you deal with Good Friend Electric, you're dealing with a good friend.
